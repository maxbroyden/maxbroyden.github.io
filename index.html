Max Broyden

College student, Blacksburg townie, aspiring writer, cartoonist in spare time. Generally willing to say yes.

About Max

Max was born in Russia before getting carried over to the States when he was less than a year old. He gained a deep passion for storytelling and art early on, and he's currently majoring in Creative Writing and Literature and Language at Virginia Tech. It's pretty convenient too, considering how his hometown happens to be Blacksburg. Max loves Cheez-Its, dark chocolate, superheroes, and having a fun time with his friends in the 'Burg.
