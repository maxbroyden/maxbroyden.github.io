About Me

I was born in Russia before getting carried over to the States when I was less than a year old. I gained a deep passion for storytelling and art early on, and I'm currently majoring in Creative Writing and Literature and Language at Virginia Tech. It's pretty convenient too, considering how my hometown happens to be Blacksburg. I love Cheez-Its, dark chocolate, superheroes, movies, TV, and having a fun time with my friends in the 'Burg.
